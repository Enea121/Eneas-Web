Our Story

Nike is a multinational corporation that is engaged in the design, development, manufacturing, and worldwide marketing and sales of footwear, apparel, equipment, accessories, and services. The company is headquartered near Beaverton, Oregon, in the Portland metropolitan area. It is one of the world's largest suppliers of athletic shoes and apparel and a major manufacturer of sports equipment, with revenue in excess of US$24.1 billion in its fiscal year 2012 (ending May 31, 2012). As of 2021, it employed more than 73,000 people worldwide.



Our Mission

At Nike, we believe in the power of sport to move the world forward. Our mission is to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete* in the world. (*If you have a body, you are an athlete.) We strive to create groundbreaking products, services, and experiences that push the boundaries of sport and help athletes of all levels achieve their full potential.



Our Team

Our team is made up of passionate athletes, designers, innovators, and problem-solvers who are dedicated to creating products that empower and inspire athletes around the world. We value diversity, creativity, and collaboration, and we work hard to create a culture where everyone feels valued and supported.
